6 Key Execution Protocols

1. Enrolment (Form 6)

The primary gateway for citizens reaching 18 years. Requires valid proof of residence and age. Register online at the ECI portal or via the Voter Helpline app.

2. Ethical Voting Oath

A moral commitment to cast a vote without fear or favor, resisting all inducements. This pillar ensures the integrity of the collective mandate.

3. Identity Verification

You must carry your EPIC (Voter ID) or one of the 12 alternative IDs like Aadhaar, PAN, or Passport to the booth to prove your identity to the PO.

4. EVM/VVPAT Protocol

Press the blue button once for your candidate. Verify that the VVPAT paper slip displays the correct name and symbol for 7 seconds behind the glass.

5. Proxy & Postal Voting

Facilitating democratic access for Service Voters (ETBPS), PwD citizens, and Senior Citizens (80+) who cannot visit a polling booth physically.

6. Model Code (MCC)

Guidelines to ensure a level playing field. Candidates are barred from using official positions or government funds for partisan campaigning once announced.
